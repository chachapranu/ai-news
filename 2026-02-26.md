### Claude AI Exploited in Mexican Government Breach
A hacker exploited Anthropic's Claude AI chatbot to carry out attacks against Mexican government agencies, successfully stealing sensitive tax and voter information. This marks a concerning escalation in AI-assisted cyber attacks targeting critical government infrastructure.

**Source:** [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-02-25/hacker-used-anthropic-s-claude-to-steal-sensitive-mexican-data)

### Russian Threat Actor Compromises 600+ FortiGate Devices Using AI
A Russian-speaking threat actor leveraged commercial generative AI services to compromise over 600 FortiGate devices across 55 countries between January 11 and February 18, 2026. The attack exploited exposed management ports and weak credentials rather than vulnerabilities, demonstrating how AI is accelerating traditional attack vectors.

**Source:** [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/02/ai-assisted-threat-actor-compromises.html)

### AI Assistants Exploited as C2 Proxies
Security researchers demonstrated that AI assistants with web browsing capabilities, including Microsoft Copilot and xAI Grok, can be abused as command-and-control (C2) proxies, opening new attack vectors that bypass traditional security controls.

**Source:** [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/02/researchers-show-copilot-and-grok-can.html)

### Cline CLI Supply Chain Attack Targets Developers
On February 17, 2026, the AI-powered coding assistant Cline CLI was compromised via an unauthorized npm publish token, causing OpenClaw AI agent to be stealthily installed on developers' machines. This represents a new category of supply chain attacks targeting AI development tools.

**Source:** [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/02/infostealer-steals-openclaw-ai-agent.html)

### AI Compressing Cyber Attack Timelines
New research shows AI is dramatically compressing cyberattack timelines, with 32% of flaws now exploited on day-zero and phishing attacks up 1,265%, forcing organizations to fundamentally shift their defense strategies.

**Source:** [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/02/from-exposure-to-exploitation-how-ai.html)
