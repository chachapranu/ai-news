Google observed the North Korea-linked threat actor UNC2970 using its Gemini AI model to conduct reconnaissance on targets, including synthesizing OSINT and profiling high-value targets.

**Source:** [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/search/label/artificial%20intelligence)

New infostealer malware has begun stealing OpenClaw AI agent configuration files and gateway tokens, marking "a significant milestone in the evolution of infostealer behavior: the transition from stealing browser credentials to harvesting the 'souls' and identities of personal AI agents".

**Source:** [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/2026/02/infostealer-steals-openclaw-ai-agent.html)
